Compatibility

Check your vehicle and key fob.

Choose a year, make, and model from the catalogue below. We show exact battery-size information where it is available and a cautious check-your-fob result where the remote style can vary.

A yellow result means either the listed fob uses CR2032 but remains untested with Lo-Key, or the battery varies by fob style and must be checked. A red result means the listed fob uses a different battery. Because key-fob styles can vary within the same year and model, always confirm the marking on the battery inside your key.

Not listed? A CR2032 marking is a good starting point, but compartment clearance, contacts, and pulse-current requirements still need to be validated.

Safety & limitations

One layer of security - not a promise that a vehicle cannot be stolen.

Product Warning

Lo-Key is designed to reduce exposure to relay attacks by removing power from an idle key fob. It does not stop other theft methods that do not depend on the key’s wireless response.

Coin batteries can cause severe injury if swallowed. Keep the product and any removed battery away from children. Do not recharge, crush, puncture, short-circuit, disassemble, or expose it to fire or excessive heat.
