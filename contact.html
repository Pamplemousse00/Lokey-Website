How can we help?

Questions about compatibility, pre-orders, installation, or an existing order belong in one place.

Email support

Send questions to support@lokey.ca. Include your vehicle year, make, model, and the battery marking inside the key fob when asking about compatibility.

For order support, include the purchaser name and order number. Do not send payment-card details or passwords by email.

Product and compatibility

Tell us the exact vehicle and key-fob battery size. Photos of the battery compartment may help, but remove keys, addresses, serial numbers, and other personal details from the image.

Orders and delivery

Include your order number and the email used at checkout. Shipping estimates, tracking, and address changes are handled according to our Shipping & delivery policy.

Returns and warranty

Before sending anything back, contact support for return instructions. Review the Returns & refunds policy and Limited warranty.

Privacy requests

Requests to access, correct, or delete personal information can be sent to the same address with “Privacy request” in the subject line. See our Privacy policy.
